Mummy J’s Treats

Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Mummy J’s Treats

This name is the actual brand name used by the business in Uyo, Nigeria. It is warm, memorable, and reflects the affectionate local nickname “Mummy J” given to the owner, Marylou. The name instantly communicates homemade quality and care.

Possible domain (for future): mummyjtreats.com or mummyjs.com.ng

2. Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official online presence for Mummy J’s Treats, a beloved local food and pastry business in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. It will:

  • Showcase the full menu of cakes, pastries, small chops, parfaits, drinks, and dine-in meals.
  • Highlight event catering and birthday/wedding services.
  • Enable customers to place orders and request home delivery.
  • Share the story of the owner (Marylou) and build community trust.
  • Provide clear contact information and operating hours.

3. Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  • “What kinds of cakes do you make, and can I see pictures and prices?”
  • “Do you deliver to my area in Uyo, and how much does delivery cost?”
  • “Can you cater my wedding/birthday with small chops and a custom cake?”
  • “What are your opening hours and exact location?”
  • “How do I place an order right now?”

4. Color Scheme

  • Primary – #E07A5F (Warm Orange) → Headings, buttons, navigation background, accents
  • Secondary – #F4A261 (Soft Yellow-Orange) → Hover states, highlights, borders
  • Background – #FFF8F0 (Creamy off-white) → Main page background
  • Text – #3D405B (Deep Navy) → Body text for excellent contrast
  • White (#FFFFFF) → Card backgrounds and light sections

5. Typography

  • Headings – Playfair Display (serif, elegant, used for warmth and Nigerian hospitality feel)
  • Body text, buttons, navigation – Poppins (clean, modern sans-serif, excellent readability)
  • Maximum of two font families to keep the site fast and consistent.
